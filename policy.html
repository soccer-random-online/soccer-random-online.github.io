🐇 Poor Bunny · Privacy Policy 🍃

Effective from: April 13, 2026 · Last updated: April 2026

Poor Bunny (accessible at https://soccer-random-online.github.io/) values your digital privacy as much as a bunny values its carrot stash. This policy describes how we handle your information when you visit our cozy corner of the internet.

🔹 1. Information we collect

We do not actively collect personal identifiers like your name, email, or address. However, like most websites, our Bitbucket hosting may log standard server data: your IP address (anonymised in analytics where possible), browser type, referring pages, and timestamps. This helps us understand Poor Bunny traffic and improve your experience — no tracking cookies for creepy profiling.

🍪 2. Cookies & local data

Poor Bunny does not use tracking or advertising cookies. Essential functional storage (like remembering theme preferences) may be stored locally on your device, but only if explicitly triggered. You can always clear your browser data — the bunny won’t mind.

🔗 3. Third-party links & services

Our pages may link to external resources (open-source tools, references). Those sites have independent privacy policies. Poor Bunny is not responsible for their content or data handling. Please review their policies before interacting.

🛡️ 4. Data security & retention

Since we don’t store personal data, there’s little to compromise. Server logs are retained for a limited period (usually up to 30 days) for security and debugging, then automatically rotated. We take reasonable measures to protect any transient data from unauthorised access.

👧 5. Children's privacy

Poor Bunny is a general audience site and does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with data, contact us (via our repo issues) and we’ll remove it promptly.

✉️ 6. Your choices & rights

You have the right to browse anonymously. Since we hold no PII, there’s nothing to delete or correct. If you have privacy concerns, you can contact the site maintainer through the Bitbucket project page. For EU residents: GDPR compliance is respected — no active profiling, no data sale.

🌱 7. Changes to this policy

If Poor Bunny ever changes how it handles data, we will update this page with a fresh revision date. Keep hopping back occasionally to stay informed. Continued use of the site implies agreement with this policy.
